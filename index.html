About Ann M Ohern

Meet Ann Marie Ohern, a seasoned and accomplished music teacher hailing from East Hanover, New Jersey. Her musical journey began at a remarkably young age when, at just 10 years old, she took to the stage to sing for thousands of captivated audience members. The performance marked the inception of what would become an extraordinary career in music.

One of the defining moments of Ann Marie's early career was her debut alongside the legendary Beverly Sills in the production of "The Ballad of Baby Doe." This experience not only showcased her exceptional talent but also paved the way for her to work with some of the most prominent figures in the music industry.

For half a century, Ann Marie Ohern has been dedicated to the art of teaching music, sharing her passion, knowledge, and expertise with countless students. With a vast array of experiences and insights gained over the years, she has become a true guiding light for aspiring musicians.

Throughout her illustrious career, Ann Marie has had the privilege of collaborating with a host of notable personalities, further enriching her musical prowess. Some of the famous names she has worked with include Ann Elgar, a gifted composer; Frances Bible, an outstanding pianist; Lila Herbert, a renowned soprano; Placido Domingo, the iconic opera singer; and Walter Chesterton, a celebrated conductor. These partnerships have not only honed her skills but also expanded her understanding of various musical genres and styles.

Online, Ann Marie is better known as Ann Marie Wisnofsky, embracing the power of the digital world to extend her reach and impact as a music educator. Her online presence allows her to connect with students from diverse backgrounds and locations, inspiring and nurturing their musical talents through virtual piano lessons.

Additionally, her remarkable contributions to the music community have earned her a place in the esteemed Douglas Moore list, further validating her standing as a respected and influential figure in the field of music education.

As she continues her journey as a piano teacher, Ann Marie Ohern remains committed to instilling a love for music in each of her students, fostering a supportive and nurturing learning environment. Her dedication to the craft, paired with her vast experience and association with musical legends, makes her an exceptional choice for aspiring pianists seeking to explore the world of music under the guidance of a true maestro. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced musician, Ann Marie's lessons promise to be transformative, helping you unlock your full musical potential and set you on a path to excellence in the world of piano playing.

Piano Lessons

Welcome to AMO Music's Piano Lessons! In our piano lessons, you will learn how to read, finger, and play classical music with proper techniques. The lessons are structured as half-hour weekly sessions, providing regular and consistent learning opportunities. Our curriculum is tailored to suit each student's ability, and if you have experience playing a flute or any other instrument in the past, it may influence the course content. We welcome students of all ages, whether you are a beginner or have prior musical experience. The only equipment requirement for our piano lessons is a piano or keyboard. As for pricing, please contact us directly to discuss the details. Scheduling is flexible, and you can get in touch with us to find a suitable time for your lessons. Our registration process is simple, and you don't need to pay in advance. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to reach out to us. We are here to guide you on your musical journey. Join us for an enriching experience and let the magic of piano music unfold!
